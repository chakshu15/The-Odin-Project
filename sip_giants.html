Sip of the Giants

Ingredients:

  1. 33 oz Norwegian pale ale or similar craft beer
  2. 2 oz aquavit (Scandinavian spirit)
  3. Garnish: Fresh thyme sprig, lemon zest

Process:

  • Pour the craft beer into a large mug or stein.
  • Slowly add the aquavit, letting it infuse with the beer.
  • Gently stir, then garnish with a sprig of thyme and a twist of lemon zest.

Speciality

A hearty drink that giants would be proud to drink, it brings bold, earthy flavors reminiscent of the vast Norwegian landscapes. The aquavit adds a touch of mystic depth, making each sip feel larger than life.
