Thor's Hammer Slush

Ingredients:

  1. 1.5 oz vodka
  2. 1 oz lime juice
  3. 1 oz blue curaçao
  4. 2 oz energy drink (for an extra kick)
  5. 1 cup crushed ice
  6. Garnish: Lime wedge, a sprinkle of sea salt

Process:

  • a blender, add vodka, lime juice, blue curaçao, and crushed ice.
  • Blend until smooth and slushy.
  • Pour into a frosty glass and top with the energy drink for an extra hit.
  • Garnish with a lime wedge and a touch of sea salt.

Speciality

This powerful, refreshing slush hits hard, just like a swing of Mjolnir, with a shockingly intense energy boost.
